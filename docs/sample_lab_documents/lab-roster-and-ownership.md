## Escalation path

1. Ask a peer in the `#lab-general` Slack channel.
2. If unresolved, ping the equipment or protocol owner directly.
3. For safety incidents, contact Daniel Okafor first, then PI.
4. For anything that affects multiple people or stops work, escalate to
   Sara Kowalski.
5. For science direction questions, ask your project lead and copy Dr.
   Vasquez.

## When ownership changes

Roles rotate annually for the imaging, data, tissue culture, and
ordering leads. If you take over a role, update this document the same
day, then announce in `#lab-general`. Stale ownership maps are how new
members end up emailing people who left two years ago — please keep
this current.
