# Microscope SOP — Leica DMi8 (Room B-204)

This document covers booking, training, and daily operating procedure for the
shared Leica DMi8 inverted fluorescence microscope in room B-204. It supersedes
all prior SOPs and informal Slack instructions. The current version owner is
Priya Mehta (mehta@example.lab).

## Booking procedure

The microscope is reserved through the shared Google Calendar named
`bench-microscopes-bookings`. Reservations must be made at least 24 hours in
advance for any session longer than 30 minutes. Same-day reservations under
30 minutes are allowed if the calendar slot is open.

After booking, send a short message in the `#imaging` Slack channel naming
your sample type, the channel(s) you intend to image, and your expected
finish time. This lets the next user prepare and prevents back-to-back
conflicts when one session runs long.

## Required training before first solo use

You must complete all three steps before using the microscope unsupervised:

1. Read this SOP end to end.
2. Watch the 12-minute internal walkthrough video (link in the Notion page
   "Imaging onboarding").
3. Run one full session under direct supervision of Priya Mehta or another
   trained user. The supervisor signs off in the shared "Microscope users"
   spreadsheet.

The supervised session should include startup, sample loading, channel
selection, image capture, and full shutdown. Do not skip the shutdown
walkthrough — most service calls in the last year were caused by improper
shutdown sequences.

## Daily operating procedure

1. Log your session start time in the paper logbook on the desk next to the
   microscope. Include your name, sample type, and channels used.
2. Power on the lamp housing and wait three minutes before opening shutters.
3. Use only the immersion oil bottle labeled "DMi8 — leave on bench". Do not
   substitute oil from other instruments.
4. After your session, lower the objective fully, close all shutters, power
   down the lamp, and cover the stage. Wipe any oil residue with a fresh
   lens tissue (never a Kimwipe).

## Calibration and maintenance

Beam alignment is checked monthly by Priya Mehta. If you notice uneven
illumination, a soft-focused channel, or unusual artifacts, log it in the
"DMi8 issues" sheet and notify Priya — do not attempt alignment yourself.

Annual factory service is scheduled for the second week of September.
Reservations during that window will be blocked on the calendar.

## Failure points to watch for

- Forgetting the three-minute lamp warm-up causes uneven illumination.
- Skipping the shutdown sequence damages the shutter mechanism.
- Using non-DMi8 immersion oil causes long-term coating wear on the
  objectives.
